Industries Served

Engineering better, safer, more efficient ways to accomplish objectives through custom access solutions.

Universal engineers and fabricates custom access solutions for multiple industries. Our engineering team has the experience needed to meet your industry-specific challenges down to the last detail. We partner with your team to provide reliable, cost-effective custom access solutions to accomplish your objectives.

Industries We Serve

Aviation & Aerospace

Our team works closely with airlines, aerospace manufacturers, and space transportation companies to develop custom access and fall prevention solutions that become an integral part of production and maintenance functions.

Bridge Construction

Our engineering team has extensive experience designing scaffold access for the bridge construction industry. We partner with your bridge engineers to develop custom scaffold systems meeting your diverse access needs.

Commercial Construction

Universal is the ideal partner for any size company in the commercial construction industry. We provide the highest-quality equipment, backed by industry-leading customer support.

Industrial Maintenance

Industrial Maintenance From custom industrial work platforms and walkway scaffolding to scaffold ladders and fall protection, Universal has the engineering, manufacturing and customer support team to achieve your industrial access requirements

Infrastructure

We partner with local, state and national government contractors providing custom access solutions and scaffold engineering services for infrastructure projects such as bridge construction, airport expansion, power plants and public city structures.

Military & Defense

With decades of experience manufacturing domestic and military-spec equipment, designing fall protection systems and engineered access solutions, Universal is the ideal partner for any government entity or contractor in the defense industry.
